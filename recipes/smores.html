S'more Recipe

Description

A s'more is a confection consisting of toasted marshmallow and chocolate sandwiched between two pieces of graham cracker. S'mores are popular in the United States and Canada, and traditionally cooked over a campfire.

Ingredients

  • Graham crackers
  • Chocolate
  • Marshmallows

Steps

  1. Place marshmallow and chocolate between two graham crackers.
  2. Put everything on a metal skewer.
  3. Place skewer near an open flame or another heating contraption.
